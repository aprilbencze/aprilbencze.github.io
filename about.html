I love not man the less, but nature more.

- Lord Byron

Growing up in a coastal town on Vancouver Island, Canada, April has been surrounded by natural beauty from a young age. Her enthusiasm for wild places and beings has only increased with time, playing the dominant role in her life and pushing her to document and explore Canada's west coast both above and underwater.

Working for Pacific Wild as their multimedia coordinator has enabled April to use her diverse skillset as a photographer, videographer, producer, writer and diver to draw awareness to pressing coastal conservation issues. Passion for the coast is what drives her, and it is through her lens and words that she seeks to inspire the same love for nature in others.
